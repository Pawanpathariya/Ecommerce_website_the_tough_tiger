About Us

The Tough Tiger

Welcome to The Tough Tiger, your ultimate destination for high-quality leather goods. Established with a passion for premium craftsmanship and timeless design, we specialize in offering top-notch leather products, from stylish leather jackets , and accessories.


Our journey began with Shashank Gupta, a seasoned entrepreneur with a keen eye for detail and an unwavering commitment to quality. With years of experience in the leather industry, Shashank set out to create a brand that stands for durability, style, and exceptional craftsmanship.


We are proudly associated with Anant Enterprises, our trusted trading partner, which has been instrumental in sourcing only the finest leather materials for our products. Their expertise ensures that every item we offer is crafted to perfection and designed to stand the test of time.
At The Tough Tiger, we believe in offering our customers more than just leather products—we offer a lifestyle defined by resilience, strength, and sophistication. Every product we make is a testament to our dedication to quality, ensuring that you not only look good but feel good too.
Explore our collection and discover why we are the brand you can trust for all your leather needs. Thank you for choosing The Tough Tiger—where strength meets style.
